Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

At RentAuto Pro, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our car rental services or visit our website.

This policy applies to all users of our services, including website visitors, customers, and anyone who interacts with our business.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • • Full name and contact details
  • • Email address and phone number
  • • Driver's license information
  • • Credit card and payment details
  • • Date of birth and age verification

Usage Information

  • • Website browsing behavior
  • • Device and browser information
  • • IP address and location data
  • • Rental history and preferences
  • • Customer service interactions

How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery

Processing reservations, managing rentals, and providing customer support

Communication

Sending booking confirmations, updates, and important service notifications

Security & Verification

Verifying identity, preventing fraud, and ensuring safe transactions

Improvement

Analyzing usage patterns to enhance our services and user experience

Data Protection & Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

Encryption

SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission

Secure Storage

Protected servers with limited access controls

Access Control

Regular audits and employee training programs

Third-Party Disclosures

Limited Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited data only in these specific circumstances:

  • With payment processors to handle transactions securely
  • With insurance companies when required for claims processing
  • With legal authorities when required by law or to protect our rights
  • With trusted service providers who assist in our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality and security

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors use our website
